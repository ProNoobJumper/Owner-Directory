# .impeccable.md — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Two audiences: (1) business owners across India listing their ventures to gain visibility; (2) customers/searchers finding local professionals across any industry. Both visit the homepage. Owners interact with admin forms. Context is discovery and trust-building — users need confidence in what they find.

### Brand Personality
**Three words:** Premium · Trusted · Vibrant

The interface should feel like a refined Indian business publication — authoritative and credible, but warm and full of life. Not cold corporate. Not chaotic bazaar. Think: a well-edited magazine for Indian entrepreneurship.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Editorial magazine meets premium marketplace. Bold typography, strong hierarchy, intentional use of colour as meaning (not decoration).
- **Theme:** Light + Dark mode with toggle.
- **References:** Premium Indian brand aesthetics — think Condé Nast India, Vogue Business, or a high-end chamber of commerce directory. Structured, trustworthy, colourful without being garish.
- **Anti-references:** Generic "AI slop" — no purple-to-blue gradients, no glassmorphism cards, no neon glows, no identical rounded card grids, no gradient text on headings, no floating blobs.
- **Color:** Warm deep tones as base (deep saffron, forest green, or rich burgundy as primary accent — distinctly Indian without being cliché). Cream/warm white for light mode. Rich dark charcoal (not pure black) for dark mode. Avoid cool grays entirely.
- **Typography:** A strong serif display font (editorial weight) paired with a clean humanist sans for body. Modular scale with fluid sizing.

### Design Principles
1. **Hierarchy over decoration** — every visual element must serve clarity, not aesthetics alone.
2. **Warmth with authority** — colour and type should feel welcoming but credible. Not playful, not clinical.
3. **Editorial rhythm** — vary card sizes, spacing, and composition. Avoid identical repeating units.
4. **India-grounded** — subtle cultural warmth in palette and texture without resorting to stereotypes.
5. **Earned complexity** — light mode feels airy and structured; dark mode feels rich and immersive. Both must feel intentional, not just inverted.
